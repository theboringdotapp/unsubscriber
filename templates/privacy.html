Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes our policies and procedures on the collection, use and disclosure of your information when you use the Unsubscriber service and tells you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

We use your personal data to provide and improve the service. By using the service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

What Data We Collect

Google Account Information

When you log in with Google, we receive basic profile information such as your name and email address. This information is used solely for authentication purposes and to display your email address during the unsubscription process.

Email Data

To provide our unsubscription service, we scan your Gmail inbox for subscription emails and extract unsubscribe links. This data is:

  • Processed in real-time during your session only
  • Never stored permanently on our servers
  • Only used for identifying and processing unsubscribe requests

Usage Data

We collect minimal usage data such as your IP address and browser information. This data is used for:

  • Maintaining the security of our service
  • Optimizing performance and user experience
  • Diagnosing technical issues

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Authenticating your account via Google OAuth
  • Scanning your inbox to identify subscription emails
  • Processing unsubscribe requests on your behalf
  • Improving the functionality and performance of our service

Data Security

We take data security seriously:

  • All data is processed using secure HTTPS connections
  • We use OAuth 2.0 for authentication with minimal required permissions
  • Email data is processed in-memory and discarded after use
  • No email content or user data is stored long-term

Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal necessary cookies for:

  • Session management (required for authentication)
  • Storing user preferences (like dark/light mode)

We do not use third-party analytics or tracking cookies.

Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with:

  • Google APIs (for Gmail access and authentication)
  • Vercel (for hosting and infrastructure)

Each of these services has their own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 13, we take steps to remove that information.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access information we have about you
  • Right to rectification of incorrect data
  • Right to erasure of your data
  • Right to restrict or object to processing
  • Right to data portability

Since we don't store your data permanently, many of these rights are automatically fulfilled.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
